Strogonoff de Frango

Background

Strogonoff de Frango is a Brazilian dish that is a variation of the traditional Russian dish. It is made with chicken instead of beef and is typically served with rice and fries. The dish is creamy and flavorful, with a rich sauce made from ketchup, mustard, and cream cheese. It is a popular comfort food in Brazil and is often served at family gatherings and celebrations.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 1 onion
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon of ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon of mustard
  • 1 tablespoon of cream cheese
  • 1 can of cream milk

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and saut√© the onion until golden brown.
  2. Add the chicken breast and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add the ketchup, mustard, and cream cheese and stir until well combined.
  4. Pour in the cream milk and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Serve with rice and fries.
